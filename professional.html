I am apart of a Cal Poly, Graphic Communication chapter called TAGA, also known as the Technical Association of the Graphic Arts.

Our chapter writes, prints, binds and manufactures several technical journals that get presented then judged by industry professionals at a specific location each year. This year, we had 7 members of our chapter travel to Memphis Tennessee to represent our school at the TAGA Conference.

TAGA raised over $5,000 and spent over 150 hours working on our “Evolution of Print” themed journal. In 2016, our Cal Poly chapter took second place at the TAGA national conference!

Next year, I will take over as TAGA's Production Coordinator. As Production Coordinator, I will be in charge of scheduling our journal's production runs and being the main form of communication between our members, our professors, and our advisors throughout the year.
